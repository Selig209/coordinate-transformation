Understanding Datum Transformations

What is a Datum?

A geodetic datum is a reference system for defining positions on Earth's surface. Different datums use different ellipsoid models and origin points.

Ghana's Coordinate Systems

  • WGS84: Global standard, used by GPS satellites
  • Ghana National Grid: Based on Clarke 1880 ellipsoid and Accra Datum
  • UTM: Universal system, Ghana spans zones 30N and 31N

Transformation Accuracy

Transformation accuracy depends on several factors:

  • Quality of transformation parameters (7-parameter Helmert transformation)
  • Local datum deformation
  • Survey methodology and equipment accuracy
Transformation Expected Accuracy Application
WGS84 ↔ UTM < 1 meter Engineering surveys, cadastral mapping
WGS84 ↔ Ghana Grid 1-5 meters National surveying, land registration
